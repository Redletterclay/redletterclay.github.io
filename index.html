Welcome to Red Letter Clay —
Handmade Pottery from Chicago, IL.

My first profound experience with handmade ceramics happened before I even touched clay in a studio, at WAD Cafe in Osaka, Japan.

Inside, a tea master chose a tea bowl from shelves lined with beautiful artisan pottery for your order. After hours, the cafe served as a gallery and marketplace supporting the local ceramic artists who made the cafe's pottery.

Tableware made by hand appeals not only to the eyes but to ones tactile senses when being picked up and used.

After working with clay for 4 years, I often think back to that day in Osaka. My hope as a potter is to create pieces that make your daily ceremonies more special like your morning cup of coffee, tending to your houseplants, or meals with family and friends.

It's not just potters who keep handmade ceramics alive, it's the people who use and enjoy them.

Davey Ball
